07 / 14
The Framework
The C.A.R.E. Framework
C
Context
Set the scene. Who are you? What's the situation?
"I am a CS student building my first portfolio..."
A
Action
What exactly do you want it to do?
"Create a landing page that includes..."
R
Role
Tell AI who to be.
"Act as a professional web designer..."
E
Expected Output
Format, tone, style, length.
"...modern, mobile-friendly, dark theme, 5 sections"
08 / 14
Bad vs Good
Same task. Different result.
❌ Weak Prompt
"Make me a website"
Generic. Vague. Forgettable output.
✓ Strong Prompt
"Act as a professional web designer. Create a portfolio website for a Filipino CS student looking for their first internship. Include About, Skills, Projects, and Contact sections. Use a clean, modern dark theme with yellow accents."
Specific. Contextual. Professional output.
The difference isn't talent. It's how you ask.
